Spaghetti

Description:

This recipe has been handed down from my mother. It is a family favorite and will not be replaced! (Definite husband pleaser!)

Ingredients:

  • 1 pund ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 small green bell pepper, diced
  • 1 (28 ounce) can diced tomatoes
  • 1 (16 ounce) can tomato paste
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 tespoon black pepper

Steps:

  1. Combine ground beef, onion, garlic, and green pepper in a alrge saucepan. Cook and stir until meat is brown and vegetables are tender. Drain grease.
  2. Stir diced tomatoes, tomato sauce, and tomato paste into the pan. Season with oregano, basil, salt and pepper. Simmer spaghetti sauce for 1 hour, stirring occasionally.
